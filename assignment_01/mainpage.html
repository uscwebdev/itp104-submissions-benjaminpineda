History of Standard Schnauzers

The Standard Schnauzer is the original breed of the three breeds of Schnauzer. Despite its wiry coat and general appearance, it's not related to the British terriers. Originating in Germany, this breed was primarily used as a guard dog and rat catcher. With its strong sense of duty and watchful eye, the Standard Schnauzer quickly became a beloved companion and protector of families, especially in rural areas. The breed's loyal demeanor and versatile skills made it indispensable on farms and in households alike.
