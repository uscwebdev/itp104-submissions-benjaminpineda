Project Summary for "Borzoi: The Russian Wolfhound" Website

About the Website

This website is dedicated to the Borzoi breed, offering detailed insights into its history, characteristics, and care. The aim is to provide a comprehensive resource for both enthusiasts and prospective owners, enhancing understanding and appreciation for this unique breed.

Key Features

  • Responsive Design: The website is designed to ensure optimal viewing across various devices, enhancing user experience and accessibility.
  • Contact Form: A contact form is included to facilitate communication, allowing visitors to easily reach out with questions or comments.
